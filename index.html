Logo/Placeholder logo

The aim of this logo is to establish a relationship between two key words; "assurance", "coverage".

Symbolically, the logo is an adaptation of an inverted 'love-heart' shape to represent the assurance and comfort associated with the compensation aspect of this project, and an umbrella to emotively show the protection and security afforded despite the environment.
Finally, to suggest the purpose of this project, the two symbols are tied together with a 'bank-note' that morphs to form the respective symbols.
